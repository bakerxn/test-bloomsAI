Accessibility & Licensing

We aim for WCAG 2.2 AA conformance and open, reusable content.

Accessibility commitments

  • Keyboard-accessible controls (selection via buttons; visible focus; one-click per category).
  • Clear labels, ARIA where appropriate, and live regions announcing key changes.
  • Color contrast ≥ 4.5:1 and selection indicated by shape/outline, not just color.
  • Responsive design with large tap targets (44×44 px minimum).
  • Plain language, concise content, and consistent navigation.
